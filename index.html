Future makers, your adventure starts here

Make The Future

We believe all children deserve access to a comprehensive digital education, one that encourages critical thinking, collaboration, and creativity. Whatever aspirations you have for your children, Rocket Coders will inspire them with a love for making, creating, and learning.


Clubs

Work your way through the academy curriculum at one of your local Rocket clubs. Learn how to hack drones, build websites, develop apps, mod minecraft and code games as you earn badges and achievements.

Holiday Camps

Turbo Boost your maker skills at one of our three day holiday and half term camps. Each camp will test your coding, hacking, and making skills to the extreme as you create amazing software, hack hardware, and develop games, art and architecture.

Private Tutoring

Make the future from your kitchen table with Rocket Tutors. We offer bespoke accelerated learning programs for individuals or groups of up to 4 students in London and Hertfordshire.

Hackathons and Maker Labs

Our Rocket:Hack-it Hackathons run during holidays and weekends. Join guest programmers, developers, and speakers and team up with friends to hack the future on our one day events.

Industry Experienced Team

The Rocket Team is made up of experienced and passionate developers and makers who all hold a minimum of an MA or MSC in a computing related field. Fully insured and DBS checked, your little Rockets are in safe hands.

Enhanced and Modern Curriculum

You wouldn't learn to fly in an out of date rocket ship - so why learn to code in an irrelevant programming language. At Rocket Coders we teach bleeding edge, industry relevant languages, environments, and hardware. Javascript, HTML5, Ruby, and Swift are all part of our carefully constructed and continually reviewed curriculum

Community Conscious

Every child deserves access to a creative coding education and at Rocket Coders we are committed to working with local communities, charities, and government to ensure all children, regardless of their gender, culture, or socioeconomic background, get to play a role in making the future. Get in touch today if you would like to partner with us in our mission or provide a scholarship for a child in your area.
